ASSET PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Asset Purchase Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 15, 2024, by and between Meridian Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Buyer"), and Cascade Partners Inc., a California corporation ("Seller").

ARTICLE 1 — PURCHASE PRICE

The Buyer agrees to purchase from the Seller, and the Seller agrees to sell to the Buyer, substantially all of the assets of the Seller's Digital Infrastructure Division for a total purchase price of $2,750,000 (Two Million Seven Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars), payable as follows:

(a) $1,500,000 in cash at closing;
(b) $750,000 in a secured promissory note due within 18 months of closing;
(c) $500,000 held in escrow for 12 months pending satisfaction of indemnification obligations.

ARTICLE 2 — CLOSING DATE

The closing of the transaction shall occur on or before April 30, 2024, at the offices of Sullivan & Kraft LLP, 200 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10166.

ARTICLE 3 — REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

The Seller represents and warrants that: (i) it has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement; (ii) the assets are free and clear of all liens and encumbrances; (iii) there is no pending or threatened litigation affecting the assets; and (iv) all financial statements provided to Buyer are accurate and complete in all material respects.

ARTICLE 4 — CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all non-public information received from the other party in connection with this transaction. This confidentiality obligation shall survive for a period of three (3) years following the closing date. Disclosure shall be permitted only to directors, officers, employees, and professional advisors who have a need to know.

ARTICLE 5 — NON-COMPETE

For a period of two (2) years following the closing date, Seller shall not directly or indirectly engage in any business that competes with the Digital Infrastructure Division within the United States.

ARTICLE 6 — TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement by written notice if: (a) the other party materially breaches any representation, warranty, or covenant and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of notice; or (b) the closing has not occurred by June 30, 2024.

ARTICLE 7 — GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.
